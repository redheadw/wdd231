About Chat Connect

Learn how Chat Connect works, our values, and how we protect our community.

Our Mission

Chat Connect was created to provide a friendly, welcoming space for people to connect through conversation. Whether you are looking to chat locally or meet people across the United States, our goal is to make online conversations simple, safe, and enjoyable.

We focus on positive community interaction, respectful dialogue, and easy discovery of chatrooms that match your interests.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Chat Connect free to use?

Yes. Chat Connect is free to browse and join chatrooms. Additional features may be added in the future.

How do local chatrooms work?

Local chatrooms are based on general location data and allow you to connect with people within approximately 250 miles. You may choose USA-wide rooms instead.

Do I need to share my exact location?

No. Location sharing is optional and only used to improve chatroom discovery.

How is safety handled?

Chat Connect encourages respectful behavior and provides tools to help keep conversations welcoming for everyone.

Privacy Policy

Chat Connect values your privacy. We do not sell personal information or share it with third parties for marketing purposes.

Any information collected is used only to support site functionality, such as remembering preferences or improving user experience.

Terms of Use

By using Chat Connect, you agree to participate respectfully and follow community guidelines. Harassment, abuse, or misuse of the platform is not permitted.

Chat Connect reserves the right to update these terms as the platform evolves.
